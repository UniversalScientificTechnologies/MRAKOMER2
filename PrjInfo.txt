Mrakomer (Clouds sensor) 

[InfoShortDescription.cs]
Mrakoměr
  
[InfoLongDescription.en]
Cloudsmeter is a robotics device for measurement the clouds coverage. This device is especially constructed for protection of robotics telescopes against rain and snow.
